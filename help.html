Frequently Asked Questions

Getting started with Lovart is very simple:

  1. Visit our website and click "Get Started"
  2. Choose a subscription plan that suits you (we offer a free trial)
  3. Complete the registration process
  4. Watch the quick start tutorial
  5. Start your first design project

Lovart supports multiple file formats to ensure seamless integration with your workflow:

  • Import formats: JPG, PNG, SVG, PDF, PSD
  • Export formats: JPG, PNG, SVG, PDF, GIF, MP4
  • High quality output: Supports up to 4K resolution
  • Transparent background: PNG format supports transparent background

You can cancel your Lovart subscription at any time:

  1. Log in to your Lovart account
  2. Go to "Account Settings" > "Subscription Management"
  3. Click the "Cancel Subscription" button
  4. Confirm the cancellation

Note: After cancellation, you can still use it until the end of the current billing cycle. We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee.

Lovart's AI design features are based on advanced machine learning algorithms:

  • Analyze your design needs and preferences
  • Learn best practices from massive design data
  • Provide personalized design suggestions
  • Automatically generate design elements and layouts
  • Continuously learn to improve suggestion quality
