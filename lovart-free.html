Free Trial FAQ

Is Lovart really free?

Yes, Lovart offers a forever free version with basic design features. We also offer a 14-day free trial of all features, no credit card required.

What happens after the free trial ends?

After the trial ends, you can choose to upgrade to a paid plan to continue using all features, or continue using the basic features of the free version.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. Even if you upgrade to a paid plan, you can cancel anytime in your account settings.

Are there feature limitations in the free version?

The free version has some limitations, such as monthly AI design generations, storage space, and export quality. But it is enough for personal basic design needs.

Is there a free trial for enterprise users?

Yes, enterprise users can apply for an extended 30-day free trial, including team collaboration and advanced features. Please contact our sales team.
