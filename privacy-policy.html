1. Information Collection

1.1 Information You Provide

When you use Lovart services, we may collect the following information you actively provide:

  • Registration Information: Name, email address, password
  • Payment Information: Credit card information, billing address (processed via secure payment processors)
  • Profile Information: Avatar, bio, professional information
  • Communication Content: Customer support inquiries, feedback

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you use our services, we automatically collect the following information:

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, browser information
  • Usage Data: Access times, page views, clickstream data
  • Location Information: Approximate location based on IP address
  • Performance Data: Page load times, error reports

1.3 Design Files and Project Data

To provide design services, we collect and process design files, project settings, and collaboration data you create on the platform. This data is stored on secure servers and used solely to provide you with design services.

2. Information Usage

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide Services: Provide you with AI design tools and collaboration features
  • Improve Services: Analyze usage data to improve user experience and features
  • Customer Support: Respond to your inquiries and provide technical support
  • Security Protection: Detect and prevent fraud and abusive activities
  • Marketing Communication: Send relevant marketing information with your consent
  • Legal Compliance: Comply with applicable laws and regulations

AI Model Training

We may use anonymized design data to improve our AI models. We ensure this data is strictly processed to be non-identifiable to specific individuals, and you can opt-out of data collection for AI training.

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We only share information in the following circumstances:

3.1 Service Providers

We share information with the following types of service providers:

  • Cloud Service Providers: For data storage and processing
  • Payment Processors: For processing payment transactions
  • Email Service Providers: For sending notifications and marketing emails
  • Analytics Service Providers: For analyzing usage data

3.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information as required by law in the following circumstances:

  • Responding to legal processes, government requests, or court orders
  • Protecting our rights, property, or safety
  • Investigating and preventing fraud or illegal activities
  • Protecting the personal safety of users

3.3 Business Transfers

If we are involved in a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, your information may be transferred as part of the transaction. We will notify you in advance of any such change.

4. Data Security

We take the following measures to protect your personal information:

  • Encryption Technology: Use SSL/TLS for data transmission encryption, sensitive information stored with AES-256 encryption
  • Access Control: Strictly limit employee access to personal information
  • Security Audits: Regularly conduct security audits and vulnerability scans
  • Backup Systems: Regularly backup important data to prevent data loss
  • Employee Training: Train employees on data security and privacy protection

Data Retention

We retain your information based on business needs and legal requirements. When you delete your account or no longer need our services, we will delete or anonymize your personal information in accordance with applicable laws.

5. User Rights

Under applicable data protection laws, you have the following rights:

5.1 Right to Access

You have the right to request a copy of your personal information we hold, including the types of information collected, purposes of use, and sharing recipients.

5.2 Right to Rectification

If you find that the personal information we hold is inaccurate or incomplete, you have the right to request us to correct or supplement such information.

5.3 Right to Erasure

In certain circumstances, you have the right to request us to delete your personal information, such as when the information is no longer needed for the collection purposes.

5.4 Right to Data Portability

You have the right to receive your personal information in a structured, commonly used, and machine-readable format, and have the right to transmit those data to another service provider.

5.5 Right to Object

You have the right to object to our processing of your personal information based on legitimate interests, and to object to our processing of your personal information for direct marketing purposes.

6. Cookie Policy

We use Cookies and similar technologies to improve your user experience:

6.1 Essential Cookies

These Cookies are essential for the basic functioning of the website, including user authentication, security protection, and website features. Without these Cookies, the website cannot function properly.

6.2 Performance Cookies

These Cookies help us understand how visitors interact with the website by collecting and reporting information anonymously.

6.3 Functional Cookies

These Cookies enable the website to provide enhanced functionality and personalization, such as remembering your preferences.

6.4 Cookie Management

You can manage Cookie preferences through your browser settings. Please note that disabling certain Cookies may affect the functionality of the website.

7. Children's Privacy

Lovart is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take reasonable steps to delete such information immediately. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

For users aged 13 to 18, we recommend using our services under the guidance of a parent or guardian.

8. Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When the policy changes, we will:

  • Post the updated policy on the website
  • Update the "Last Updated" date at the top of the policy
  • Notify of significant changes via email or other means
  • Obtain your consent where required by applicable law

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to understand how we protect your personal information.

Privacy Inquiries

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or need to exercise your data rights, please feel free to contact us.
